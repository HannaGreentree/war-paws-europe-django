Created by volunteers and animal lovers

War Paws International was created by volunteers and animal lovers who care deeply about animals affected by war. It has been four years since Russia’s full-scale invasion of Ukraine, and many regions still face daily attacks.

More people leave the country without their pets. More animals are left to survive on the streets near explosions, ruined buildings, bombs, and rockets.

Who this website is for

This website is mainly for people abroad who want to adopt a cat or dog from Ukraine but don’t know where to start. We also support Ukrainians who want to travel with their own pets and need guidance about documents and temporary housing options.

Our wider goal

We aim to bring more attention to the animal welfare crisis in Ukraine and support practical steps that reduce suffering while the war continues.

Ukrainians with pets

If you are leaving Ukraine with your own pet, we share guidance about documents, costs, and organisations that may help with temporary housing for animals until you find a stable place to live.

Questions & Answers

Where should I start?

Start by choosing a shelter or volunteer. We provide links to their websites and social media pages so you can verify information and contact them directly.

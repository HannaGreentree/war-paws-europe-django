CONTACT WAR PAWS EUROPE

If you are a volunteer, shelter, or a person hoping to adopt an animal from Ukraine who is experiencing difficulties adopting a specific animal, or if you suspect a shelter or individual may be involved in fraudulent activity, please choose the contact form that best fits your situation.

Fill in the form with as much detail as possible, and we will do our best to get back to you as soon as we can.

Please note: we do not accept any payments and we do not have animals for adoption ourselves. We are animal lovers who gathered information in one place to make adoption from war zones such as Ukraine easier, safer, and more transparent.
